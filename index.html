PROSPECT Lab

Pioneering Research and Organizational Solutions to Promote Equitable Care through Technology

We are the digital innovation taskforce at the Zuckerberg San Francisco General Hospital with the mission of applying technology and digital tools to improve health outcomes and equity in vulnerable and underserved populations. Our team consists of machine learning/artificial intelligence experts, healthcare providers, biostatisticians, and clinical informaticists.
