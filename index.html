PROSPECT Lab

Pioneering Research and Organizational Solutions to Promote Equitable Care through Technology

We are digital innovation taskforce at Zuckerberg San Francisco General Hospital with the mission of applying technology and digital tools to improve health outcomes and equity in vulnerable and underserved populations. We are composed of individuals with expertise in machine learning, artificial intelligence, data science, social determinants of health and electronic health record ML/AI integration.
