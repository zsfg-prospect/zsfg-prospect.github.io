Key Features

Social Risk Extraction

AI analyzes clinical notes to identify 8 social risk domains including housing, food insecurity, and safety concerns.

Assessment Gap Detection

Identifies unassessed social domains that represent care gaps based on patient diagnoses and clinical complexity.

Eligibility Detection

Automatically flags eligibility for ECM, IHSS, and SNAP Benefits programs based on CalAIM criteria.

Form Auto-Fill

Pre-populate ECM, IHSS, and SNAP referral forms with patient data and clinical evidence.
